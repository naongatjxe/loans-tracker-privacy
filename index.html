Your privacy matters. This policy explains clearly what data Loans Tracker zm collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

📋 Overview

Loans Tracker zm ("the App") is a personal finance tool that helps users record and manage loan information. The App is developed and maintained by an independent developer based in Zambia.

By using this App, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

💾 Data We Collect

The App only stores data that you manually enter, including:

  • Loan amounts, borrower names, and repayment dates
  • Notes or descriptions you attach to loan records
  • Any other financial information you choose to input

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

🚫 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity
  • We do not access your location
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts
  • We do not use advertising networks or tracking SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties

🔒 Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your information is tied to your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We strongly recommend enabling a screen lock on your device.

We have no access to your data and cannot retrieve it if your device is lost or reset.

🗑️ Deleting Your Data

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual records within the App
  • Going to your device Settings → Apps → Loans Tracker zm → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the App entirely

👶 Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
